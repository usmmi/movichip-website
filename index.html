Frequently asked questions

Where is Mövenpick Hotel Karachi located?

Mövenpick Hotel Karachi is located in the centre of Karachi's business district and adjacent to the Chief Minister and Governor's House. The hotel is also just 17 kilometres from Jinnah International Airport.

What is the address of Mövenpick Hotel Karachi?

The address of Mövenpick Hotel Karachi is:

Club Road
75530 Karachi
Pakistan

Which popular attractions are close to Mövenpick Hotel Karachi?

Mövenpick Hotel Karachi is located close to some popular attractions. These include Frere Hall, Mohatta Palace, Sea View Beach, Golf Course and Kemari Harbour.

What are some of the property amenities at Mövenpick Hotel Karachi?

Mövenpick Hotel Karachi provides the following property amenities:

  • 407 comfortable rooms and suites
  • Five dining options
  • Meeting and event facilities for up to 1,000 guests
  • Well-equipped Health Club
  • Outdoor heated swimming pool, sauna, jacuzzi and steam room
